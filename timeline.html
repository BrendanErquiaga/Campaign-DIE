Adventure Timeline

  • Owlbears, Worgs, & a prisoner!

    5/15/2016 - Cade, Oli, Tish, Turnaroth, Tyr

    After recovering from the attack on the road our heroes made their way to the Southern Outpost. Dispatching some rather feckless Hobgoblins & their Owlbear, the party soon went to sleep. The next day the adventurer's went south, looking for the source of deforestation. On the way they discovered a roaming Hobgoblin party riding on Worgs. With quick thinking, and a few explosives, the adventures killed all but one of the war party, keeping Hobgoblin 'No Name' as a prisoner. They returned to the tower with the prisoner in tow, spotting a mysterious armor clad figure on the way.

  • Intiation & The First Combat!

    5/8/2016 - Cade, Oli, Tish, Turnaroth, Tyr

    Sardan gave a brief overview of their first mission, head south & look for the cause of deforestation. So the party set off traveling southeast with Kadri, their half-elf guide. During the first night Kadri & two other members had some fun 'intiating' the party to the expedition. On the next day the party was attacked by Hobgoblins and Owlbears (oh my)!

  • Welcome to the Expedition

    5/8/2016 - Cade, Oli, Tish, Turnaroth, Tyr

    Our adventurers arrived on the Island with a thump, apparently planar travel isn't very smooth. Upon arrival they were met with a cranky old halfling by the name of Mosa. Mosa introduced the members to three important figures; Vardic, Officer of Battle, Nimrie, Officer of Survival, and Sardan, Expedition Leader.
