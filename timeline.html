Adventure Timeline

  • Huge Revelations

    6/05/2016 - Cade, Gelimer, Tish, Turnaroth, Tyr

    Aided by the tree-creature, whom Tyr had kept alive as a pet, the party was lead south, deeper & deeper into the woods. Finally they came upon a clearing in front of a canyon. The canyon was engulfed in trees & foliage, as the party approached the flora came alive. The tree-folk tried to capture Tyr yet again, they seemed committed to taking the elf. This time they brought a huge tree that towered over everyone, until Tish polymorphed Tyr into a Giant Ape.

    A few spells & slew of thunderous punches later, the party had reduced everything in the canyon to ash & kindling. They decided to head east to rest before continuing on. However before they got very far they came upon an interesting tree. When Tyr touched the tree a set of magical doors opened up to a hollow tree. The group climbed the tree only to find themselves suddenly transported to a strange room underground.

    In this strange room the party finally came face to face with the strange armored man they saw a few days past. As a twist of fate the man was actually one of the last remaining elves on Denbora Island. The elf & his strange companion, Pagk, captured the party & transported them to a kitchen. Tyr’s curiosity & Cade’s unwillingness to sit still helped the group escape the kitchen through the sewers. The adventurer’s suddenly found themselves at the bottom of a dormant volcano when a three headed flying beast started to descend upon them.

  • Monsters and Mayhem

    5/22/2016 - Cade, Gelimer, Tish, Turnaroth, Tyr

    After arriving back at the Tower our heroes proceeded to interrogate the Hobgoblin ‘No Name’. They soon learned the Hobgoblins were building an army, & that they were being sold strange beasts by an unknown source. Another part of the expedition had also run into strange mutated animals, one such creature was a basilisk morphed with a boar. The group went deep into the forest hoping to find the source of these animals, they brought a chicken to use as bait.

    On their second day in the forest the party stumbled into a clearing only to be attack by several Cockatrice & a large monstrosity with several tentacles. In the chaos of the battle, Tish was petrified by one of the Cockatrice & the group left their companion as a human statue in the middle of the forest. Thankfully Tish woke up a day later, a bit stiff but otherwise free of harm.

    After the party met back up they returned to the forest, their search took them deep into the heart of the woods. Eventually they stumbled upon a strange tree-like creature. The creature led Tyr & the party to more tree-folk who tried to capture the elf. The party fought back & killed all of the trees & another monstrosity of plant matter.

  • Owlbears, Worgs, & a prisoner!

    5/15/2016 - Cade, Oli, Tish, Turnaroth, Tyr

    After recovering from the attack on the road our heroes made their way to the Southern Outpost. Dispatching some rather feckless Hobgoblins & their Owlbear, the party soon went to sleep. The next day the adventurer's went south, looking for the source of deforestation. On the way they discovered a roaming Hobgoblin party riding on Worgs. With quick thinking, and a few explosives, the adventures killed all but one of the war party, keeping Hobgoblin 'No Name' as a prisoner. They returned to the tower with the prisoner in tow, spotting a mysterious armor clad figure on the way.

  • Intiation & The First Combat!

    5/8/2016 - Cade, Oli, Tish, Turnaroth, Tyr

    Sardan gave a brief overview of their first mission, head south & look for the cause of deforestation. So the party set off traveling southeast with Kadri, their half-elf guide. During the first night Kadri & two other members had some fun 'intiating' the party to the expedition. On the next day the party was attacked by Hobgoblins and Owlbears (oh my)!

  • Welcome to the Expedition

    5/8/2016 - Cade, Oli, Tish, Turnaroth, Tyr

    Our adventurers arrived on the Island with a thump, apparently planar travel isn't very smooth. Upon arrival they were met with a cranky old halfling by the name of Mosa. Mosa introduced the members to three important figures; Vardic, Officer of Battle, Nimrie, Officer of Survival, and Sardan, Expedition Leader.
